How AscendAI Works

1. Input Your Job Role Tell us the job role you're preparing for, and AscendAI will generate a set of tailored interview questions.

2. Practice Interviews Engage in interactive practice sessions where AscendAI will simulate the role of the interviewer, asking questions and providing real-time feedback.

3. Upload Interview Videos Record your practice interviews and upload the videos to AscendAI for analysis.

4. Emotion and Voice Analysis AscendAI analyzes your video to detect top emotions and evaluate your voice modulation, tone, and clarity.

5. Get Feedback Receive a detailed report highlighting your strengths and areas for improvement, with actionable tips to enhance your interview skills.
